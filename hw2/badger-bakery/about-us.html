About Us

Badger Bakery is a small, family-owned bakery in Madison, Wisconsin. We specialize in custom cakes, cupcakes, and cookies. We also offer a variety of breads, pastries, and other baked goods. We are open Monday through Friday from 8am to 5pm. We are closed on weekends and major holidays.

Our Story

Badger Bakery was founded in 2015 by John Doe and Jane Doe. John and Jane met while working at a local bakery. They quickly became friends and decided to open their own bakery together. They named it Badger Bakery after their love of the University of Wisconsin Badgers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the best baked goods in the Madison area. We strive to provide the best customer service and the highest quality products. We are committed to using the freshest ingredients and the best baking techniques. We are dedicated to providing a positive experience for our customers.
